# CodeX-Energy-Drink-Strategic-Blueprint-for-Market-Growth

## 1. Project Introduction
| Section          | Details                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Title            | CodeX-Energy-Drink Strategic Blueprint for Market Growth                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Context          | As part of elevating my data analytics journey through the #ResumeProjectChallenge, I analysed how CodeX Energy Drink is positioned in the competitive energy drink market and what drives its consumers.                                                                                                                                   |
| Company Overview | CodeX is a youth-focused energy drink brand targeting ages 15–30. It offers caffeine- and vitamin-based beverages for sports, study, and late-night productivity. Competing with major brands, CodeX leverages modern branding, digital-first marketing, and affordable pricing while aiming to improve taste, packaging, and distribution. |
